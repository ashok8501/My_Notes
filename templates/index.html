About

The Student Notes App is designed to help students manage their study materials in a simple and organized way. Instead of searching through scattered files, students can upload and store all their notes in one place.

Notes can be grouped by subject, making it easy to find what you need quickly. Important notes can be bookmarked for faster access, especially during exams or revision time.

With features like search, quick file viewing, and structured organization, this app helps students save time, stay focused, and keep their study materials well organized.

Overall, it acts like a personal digital notebook where everything is easy to access, manage, and reuse whenever needed.
